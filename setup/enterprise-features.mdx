# Enterprise Features

Binalyze Fleet AI provides comprehensive enterprise features designed for large-scale organizational deployment, team management, compliance requirements, and advanced administrative capabilities. These features ensure that Fleet AI can scale effectively across enterprise environments while maintaining security, governance, and operational efficiency.

**Directory Integration**:

### **Role-Based Access Control**
Advanced RBAC for complex organizational requirements:

**Custom Role Creation**:
```yaml
custom_roles:
  threat_hunter:
    permissions:
      - "yara:generate"
      - "sigma:create"
      - "osquery:execute"
      - "investigations:lead"
      - "intelligence:analyze"
    restrictions:
      - "admin:no_access"
      - "billing:no_access"
    scope: "business_unit"
  
  soc_supervisor:
    permissions:
      - "all_agents:access"
      - "teams:manage"
      - "metrics:view"
      - "reports:generate"
    restrictions:
      - "enterprise:no_config"
    scope: "department"
```

**Permission Inheritance**:
- **Role Hierarchy**: Hierarchical role inheritance with override capabilities
- **Permission Stacking**: Additive permissions from multiple role assignments
- **Least Privilege**: Automatic enforcement of least privilege principles
- **Temporary Elevation**: Time-limited privilege escalation for specific tasks

## Organizational Controls

### **Policy Management**
Enterprise-wide policy enforcement and compliance:

**Security Policies**:
- **Data Classification**: Automatic classification of generated content and conversations
- **Retention Policies**: Configurable data retention and deletion policies
- **Access Policies**: IP-based, geographic, and time-based access restrictions
- **Usage Policies**: Acceptable use policies and enforcement mechanisms

**Operational Policies**:
```yaml
enterprise_policies:
  data_retention:
    conversations: 2555  # 7 years in days
    generated_content: 2555
    audit_logs: 3653  # 10 years
    user_data: 365  # 1 year after deactivation
  
  access_controls:
    session_timeout: 3600  # 1 hour
    concurrent_sessions: 3
    ip_restrictions: true
    geo_restrictions: ["US", "CA", "EU"]
  
  content_policies:
    auto_classification: true
    watermarking: enabled
    sharing_restrictions: strict
    external_sharing: disabled
```
